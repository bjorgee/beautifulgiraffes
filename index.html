The Beauty of the Giraffe

  • Height: Towering up to 18 feet, giraffes are the tallest land animals on Earth.
  • Markings: Each giraffe has a unique coat pattern, much like human fingerprints.
  • Tongue: Their tongues, often up to 18 inches long and dark purple, help them reach for foliage high in the trees.

In Stories and Folklore

In African legends, giraffes are often symbols of foresight and intuition. From folktales to modern literature, they embody grace, mystery, and wisdom, making them an enduring subject of fascination around the world.
